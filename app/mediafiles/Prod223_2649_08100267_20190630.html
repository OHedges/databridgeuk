1. Accounting policies
Basis of preparing the financial statements
These financial statements have been prepared in accordance with the provisions of Section 1A of Financial
Reporting Standard 102 ''The Financial Reporting Standard applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland'' and the
Companies Act 2006. The financial statements have been prepared under the historical costs convention as
modified by the revaluation of certain assets.
Going Concern
Although the company has incurred losses resulting in negative retained earnings, and total liabilities exceed total
assets by £2,271 as at 30 June 2019, the director considers that the going concern basis is appropriate. The
company is supported in meeting its day to day working
capital requirements by its shareholder, Mr Jonathan Ballard. Mr Ballard has confirmed that he shall not drawn down
balances due to him such that there will be an insufficiency of working capital in the company. In addition, should
there remain an excess of net liabilities, Mr Ballard will enable the company to continue to meet its obligations to
third parties ahead of himself.
The director has considered the cash flow and profit forecasts in excess of one year forward and the strategies for
restoring the company to profitability. On the basis of this and continued support from its shareholders, the director is
of the opinion that the company will be able to continue to operate as a going concern, and therefore that this basis
of preparation of the financial statements is appropriate.
