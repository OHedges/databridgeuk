Chartered Management Accountants' report to the board of directors on the preparation of the unaudited statutory accounts of INVICTUS CARE SERVICES LTD for the year ended 31 August 2019
In order to assist you to fulfil your duties under the Companies Act 2006, we have prepared for your approval the accounts of INVICTUS CARE SERVICES LTD for the year ended 31 August 2019 which comprise of the Profit and Loss Account, the Balance Sheet and the related notes from the company's accounting records and from information and explanations you have given us.
As a practising member firm of the Chartered Institute of Management Accountants, we are subject to its ethical and other professional requirements which are detailed at http://www.cimaglobal.com.
